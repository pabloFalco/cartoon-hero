About Ralph

In Wreck-it Ralph, video game villain Ralph (voice of John C. Reilly) sets out to fulfil his dream of becoming a hero by winning a medal in a game. While his quest wreaks havoc on the arcade in which he lives, his journey leads to him becoming friends with and helping a young girl named Vanellope
